About

What is Debug Lounge?
Debug Lounge is a unique space where coffee culture meets coding. We’re a café designed for coders, tech enthusiasts, and creatives looking for the perfect environment to work, collaborate, and get inspired.

What Makes Us Different?
At Debug Lounge, we don’t just serve coffee—we create an atmosphere that fuels productivity. With cozy seating, fast Wi-Fi, and a menu curated to satisfy your taste buds, we’re more than just a place to grab a drink. We’re a community for innovators who want to code, collaborate, and caffeinate in a welcoming environment.

What Can You Expect?
Expect expertly brewed coffee, a quiet corner to focus, or a collaborative space to work with like-minded people. Our menu is designed to energize you, from rich espresso drinks to freshly brewed pots, complemented by a variety of snacks and light bites.
